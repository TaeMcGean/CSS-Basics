There are plenty of types of painting, whether its on a canvas or otherwise. the version of painting i enjoy in particular is model painting.
As for what types of models i like painting, those from the warhammer franchises are some of my favorites. Getting into the hobby was slow going.
In particular, the pricing of some of the nicer models was somewhat of a concern. When getting into the hobby, there was 1 youtube channel in particular that helped me get interested.
The first was a channel known as Play On Tabletop. This channel's whole style is presenting tabletop wargames in a quick and condensed format, and presenting information in a sussinct manner. Games of warhammer 40k will typically take multiple hours to complete, so their ability to condense it down was very helpful.
Most of all, the production quality in their videos is unmatched in the scene of tabletop games. Not only are the videos themselves impressively made, but the paint jobs showcased on different armies really gave me inspiration to work harder.
